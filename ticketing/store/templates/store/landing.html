About Us

We are a leading platform for discovering and booking tickets for a wide range of events including music concerts, exhibitions, conferences, and more.
Our mission is to provide a seamless ticketing experience, making it easy for you to attend the events you love. is a global ticketing platform that empowers people to create, discover, and attend events. We connect millions of creators and attendees around the world, making it easy to plan, promote, and sell tickets for events of all sizes.
Whether you're organizing a small gathering or a large-scale festival, K-Ticket provides the tools and resources you need to make your event a success. From music festivals and conferences to charity galas and workshops, K-Ticket offers a wide range of ticketing solutions to meet your unique needs. Our platform features intuitive tools for event creation, promotion, and management, as well as powerful analytics to help you track your event's performance. With K-Ticket, you can easily sell tickets online and at the door, manage your guest list, and communicate with your attendees.
